Work Snippets & About Me

I'm a 22-year-old creative from Malta, currently finalising my BFA in Digital Arts. Although I love branding and graphic design in general, I hope to become a UI & UX designer down the line.

As my 3 years at University are soon coming to an end, I hope that I am able to highlight my diversity of skills throughout this portfolio. I am glad that I was able to experience the basis of many different creative mediums and tools and I am happy to now specialise in Product Design.

IDENTITY DESIGN

Regina Branding

Regina insists to position itself on the market as being a Gin purveyor first above being that of a bar or a lounge. Compared to other competitors that offer a wide array of alcoholic choices, Regina does not aim to be considered as a wholesaler, but more of a destination where you can go and taste test and experiment with different drinks.

IDENTITY RE-DESIGN

UBET

In my 2nd year of University, I was tasked to create a re-design of an existing company, I decided to go with UBET, a logo gaming company which I felt needed a re-design to be able to compete with the big dogs on an international level, especially since most of them are in fact based in Malta.

PACKAGING DESIGN

Congo Treats

In my 2nd year of University, I was tasked with creating a packaging based on 3 tea-time treats products, I created the identity know as Congo Treats, along with its packaging.
